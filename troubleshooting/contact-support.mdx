## Need Help? We're Here for You

Our support team is ready to help you succeed with IllumiChat. Here are the different ways you can get assistance.

## Support Channels

### Email Support

For technical issues, account questions, and general inquiries:

**Email**: [support@illumichat.com](mailto:support@illumichat.com)

**Response Time**: Within 24 hours (usually much faster)

<Tip>
Include your account email and a detailed description of the issue for faster resolution.
</Tip>

### Live Chat

Get instant help during business hours:

- **Available**: Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- **Access**: Click the chat widget in the bottom right of any page
- **Best for**: Quick questions and immediate assistance

### Community Support

Connect with other IllumiChat users:

- **Discord**: [Join our community](https://discord.gg/illumichat)
- **GitHub**: [Report bugs and request features](https://github.com/illumichat/illumichat)
- **Documentation**: [Browse our help docs](/)

### Gather Information

To help us assist you faster, please include:

- **Account Information**: Your email address
- **Issue Description**: What you're trying to do and what's happening
- **Error Messages**: Any error codes or messages you're seeing
- **Steps Taken**: What you've already tried
- **Screenshots**: Visual evidence of the issue (if applicable)

## Priority Support

### Enterprise Customers

Enterprise plan customers receive:

- **Priority Response**: 4-hour response time
- **Dedicated Support**: Assigned support representative
- **Phone Support**: Direct phone line for urgent issues
- **Custom Training**: Personalized onboarding and training sessions

### Pro Plan Customers

Pro plan customers receive:

- **Enhanced Support**: 8-hour response time
- **Extended Hours**: Support available on weekends
- **Advanced Troubleshooting**: In-depth technical assistance

## Bug Reports

Found a bug? Help us fix it:

### What to Include

- **Steps to Reproduce**: Exact steps that cause the issue
- **Expected vs Actual**: What should happen vs what actually happens
- **Environment**: Browser, OS, and any relevant details
- **Screenshots/Videos**: Visual evidence of the bug
- **Console Logs**: Any error messages from browser console

### Submit Bug Report

1. **GitHub Issues**: [Create an issue](https://github.com/illumichat/illumichat/issues)
2. **Email**: Send to [bugs@illumichat.com](mailto:bugs@illumichat.com)
3. **Support Form**: Use the contact form below

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for improving IllumiChat?

### Submit Feature Request

1. **GitHub Discussions**: [Start a discussion](https://github.com/illumichat/illumichat/discussions)
2. **Email**: Send to [features@illumichat.com](mailto:features@illumichat.com)
3. **Community Vote**: Share in our Discord community

### What to Include

- **Use Case**: How would this feature help you?
- **Current Workaround**: How do you solve this problem now?
- **Mockups**: Visual examples if applicable
- **Priority**: How important is this to your workflow?

## Account Issues

### Billing Support

For billing and subscription questions:

- **Email**: [billing@illumichat.com](mailto:billing@illumichat.com)
- **Phone**: +1 (555) 123-4567 (Enterprise customers only)

### Account Recovery

Can't access your account?

1. **Password Reset**: Use the "Forgot Password" link
2. **Two-Factor Issues**: Contact support for 2FA recovery
3. **Account Locked**: Email support with account details

## Response Times

Our commitment to you:

| Plan | Email Response | Live Chat | Phone Support |
|------|----------------|-----------|---------------|
| Free | 48 hours | ❌ | ❌ |
| Pro | 8 hours | ✅ | ❌ |
| Enterprise | 4 hours | ✅ | ✅ |

<Info>
Response times are during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST). Weekend support available for Pro and Enterprise customers.

## Escalation Process

If you're not satisfied with the initial response:

1. **Request Escalation**: Ask the support agent to escalate your case
2. **Manager Review**: Your case will be reviewed by a support manager
3. **Direct Contact**: For urgent issues, contact [escalations@illumichat.com](mailto:escalations@illumichat.com)

## Feedback

We value your feedback to improve our service:

- **Support Quality**: Rate your support experience
- **Product Feedback**: Share your thoughts on IllumiChat
- **Documentation**: Help us improve our docs
- **General**: Any other feedback or suggestions

## Emergency Contacts

For critical issues affecting production systems:

- **Enterprise Customers**: Use your dedicated support line
- **Pro Customers**: Email [urgent@illumichat.com](mailto:urgent@illumichat.com)
- **All Customers**: Post in Discord #urgent channel

<Warning>
Emergency contacts are for critical production issues only. Please use regular support channels for non-urgent matters.
</Warning>
