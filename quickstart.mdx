## Create Your First AI Assistant

Follow this guide to build and deploy your first AI assistant with IllumiChat.

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign up for IllumiChat">
  Visit [app.illumichat.com](https://app.illumichat.com/signup) and create your account.
  
  ![Sign up page](/images/login-screenshots/create-account-page.png)
  
  Fill in your email address and create a secure password.
  
  <Check>
  You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Log in to your account">
  Log in to your IllumiChat dashboard.
  
  ![Login page](/images/login-screenshots/sign-in-sign-up-page.png)
  
  Use your email and password to access your workspace.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your email">
  After logging in, check your email for a verification link from IllumiChat.
  
  ![Email verification](/images/login-screenshots/continue-to-dashboard.png)
  
  Click the verification link in the email to activate your account.
  
  <Warning>
  If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Create your first assistant">
  Once logged in, click "Create Assistant" and fill in the basic details:
  
  ![Create Assistant](/images/assistant-screenshots/create-assistant.png)
  
  - **Name**: Give your assistant a memorable name
  - **Description**: Describe what your assistant does
  - **Personality**: Choose how your assistant should behave
  
  <Tip>
  Start with a simple, focused assistant. You can always add more features later.
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Add knowledge to your assistant">
  Upload documents or add content to give your assistant expertise:
  
  - Upload PDFs, Word docs, or text files
  - Add website URLs to crawl
  - Create custom knowledge entries
  
  <Warning>
  Make sure you have permission to use any content you upload.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Test your assistant">
  Use the built-in chat interface to test your assistant:
  
  - Ask questions about your uploaded content
  - Test different scenarios and edge cases
  - Refine responses and add more knowledge as needed
  
  <Info>
  Your assistant learns from each interaction, so the more you test, the better it becomes.
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Deploy your assistant">
  Choose how to deploy your assistant:
  
  - **Chat Widget**: Add to your website
  - **API Integration**: Connect to your applications
  - **Slack/Discord**: Deploy to team communication platforms
  
  <Check>
  Your assistant is now live and ready to help users!
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How much does IllumiChat cost?">
  We offer a free tier to get started, with paid plans starting at $49/month for advanced features and higher usage limits. See our [pricing page](/pricing) for details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What file types can I upload?">
  We support PDF, Word documents (.docx), text files (.txt), and can crawl website content. We're constantly adding support for more file types.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How secure is my data?">
  All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never use your content to train our models, and you can delete your data at any time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize the assistant's appearance?">
  Yes! You can customize colors, logos, and styling to match your brand when deploying as a chat widget.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
