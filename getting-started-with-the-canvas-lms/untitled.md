# Step 11: Send Assignment Grades to LMS

### Access the LMS Tab

To access the LMS tab, log into IllumIDesk as an instructor and access the shared grader notebook as described in [Step 3](https://docs.illumidesk.com/v2.0/docs/access-shared-grader-console).

![LMS tab](https://files.readme.io/8832d4e-lms_tab.png)

Once you click on the LMS tab, you will see the `Send` button for each graded assignment. To send grades to the LMS, click on the `Send` button.

![Click on Send button to send grades to the LMS](https://files.readme.io/f3da392-send_grades_lms_tab.png)

A success/error modal will appear letting you know if the task completed successfully. Once the task has successfully completed. log in to your LMS's grades section to view the grades by the learner. For example, with the Canvas LMS the `Gradebook` view would display grades for each student in the course.

![Posted grades in LMS course.](https://files.readme.io/146ad37-grades_posted_to_lms.png)

**This feature reduces the likelihood of error** since instructors don't have to manually export grades to formats such as CSV to then import them to the Learning Manage System. **All grades are synched with the click of a button**!
