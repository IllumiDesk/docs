## What are Credits?

Credits are consumed when your AI assistants process requests. The amount of credits used depends on:

- **Model used**: Different AI models consume different amounts of credits
- **Response length**: Longer responses use more credits
- **Knowledge base queries**: Searching through your knowledge base uses additional credits
- **MCP tool usage**: Using external integrations consumes credits

<Tip>
Most simple conversations use 1-3 credits. Complex analysis or deep research mode can use 5-10 credits per interaction.
</Tip>
