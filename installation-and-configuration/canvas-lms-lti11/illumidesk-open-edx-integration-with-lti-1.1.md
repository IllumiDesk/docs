# IllumiDesk Open edX Integration with LTI 1.1

## Overview  <a id="heading-4b0df420-c461-8307"></a>

This document describes the general steps to register for a course hosted with the edX LMS and to launch IllumiDesk from within the course using the LTI 1.1 standard.  


There are two courses available with different privacy settings:  


1. Introduction to Computer Science: email is “public” and sent to the IllumiDesk external tool. 
2. Introduction to Data Science: email is not sent to IllumiDesk and thus uses the LTI “user\_id” field for the username. 

## Register with edX Demo Environment  <a id="heading-6123c86f-fc63-09b3"></a>

1. Access the edX demo environment at [https://illumidesk.tahoe.appsembler.com/](https://illumidesk.tahoe.appsembler.com/) and click on the Register button: 

![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k6e335cf08384d452cb994b06cc540c613)

Once you have submitted the registration form, you should receive an email from “[course@appspembler.com](mailto:course@appspembler.com)” to confirm your email address. Click on the link provided in the email address to confirm your registration.  


1. Once you have logged in, click on Find Courses: 

![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k647133c91194d4b60b5b9d1abd9ab7ec2)

1. Register for one of the two courses by clicking on the course title: 

![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k6981354d8c0f84dad97316ef9c6c04d82)

1. Once you have accessed the course view, click on “Enroll in &lt;Course Title&gt;”: 

![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k6c5844024b1f34a4ab74e6e786ef3f5ba)

1. After you have enrolled for the course, you will see the “View Course” option. Click on this button: 

![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k6a212c722035845f99698c52e56c8317a)

## Launch the IllumiDesk Application  <a id="heading-2bfcf0d7-0952-c347"></a>

Users, as enrolled students, have access to the course content. Click on “Expand All” to see all units and the IllumIDesk launcher:  


![](https://support.illumidesk.com/DocsDisplay?zgId=679933615&mode=inline&blockId=bl2k6d3d741c3954f4cc78605ac7cf22a1293)

Click on the IllumIDesk button to launch the Jupyter Notebook in a new browser window.
